  2. People believe that money plants radiate positive energy and induce calmness and serenity.
  3. Money plants absorb any negativity in the house. They also help reduce anxiety and stress and allow us to sleep peacefully. A money plant brings positive vibes and good luck to a home

Monstera Deliciosa
Paradise

Monstera is a tropical plant from the Araceae family. Its name, as one might guess, comes from the Latin meaning"abnorma" because of its odd-looking, perforated leaves that are often punched through with holes

Benefits
  1. It can purify the air and is useful in humidifying air conditions
  2. Its fruit is full of vitamin C, proteins, some vitamin B, calcium, phosphorous, and no fat but it is poisonous if it does not fully ripen
  3. It can be a cure for different diseases and health problems such as arthritis and insect or snake bites
